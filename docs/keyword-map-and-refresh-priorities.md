## How to Read This Document

Each page is assigned:
- **Primary keyword**: the one term this page should own.
- **Secondary cluster**: supporting terms to weave into headings and body.
- **Intent**: what job the searcher is trying to do.
- **Brand territory**: which of the 5 strategic territories this serves.
- **Refresh priority**: HIGH (do now), MEDIUM (next sprint), LOW (monitor).
- **Recommended action**: what to do with this page.
