## SERP Patterns Found

The strongest ranking pages share these patterns:

1. Answer-first intros that define the problem quickly.
2. Table-heavy diagnosis and action frameworks.
3. Clear workflows rather than generic advice.
4. Source-backed claims, especially for Google guidance and current SEO data.
5. Visible author or brand authority.
6. Internal paths into related guides, tools, or commercial pages.
7. FAQ-style coverage of follow-up questions.
