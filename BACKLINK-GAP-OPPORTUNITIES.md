## Excluded sources

- Toxic crawl sources such as `*-links-bhs.xyz` were removed.
- Generic hosts, unrelated foreign-language sites, and implausible sources such as `apple.com` were excluded because the crawl does not provide enough page-level evidence.
- Broad sources linking to `seo.com` were not treated as immediate prospects when the relationship appeared unattainable or irrelevant to the AI SEO focus.

## Data limitations

- Moz and Bing Webmaster backlink APIs are not configured.
- The bundled Common Crawl web-graph lookup timed out and its current parser cannot resolve numeric graph edges into referring-domain names.
- Crawly data is directional and monthly, not exhaustive. Before outreach, manually verify the competitor link and the target site's submission/editorial policy.
